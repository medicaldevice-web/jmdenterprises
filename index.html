Biochemistry Analyzer

Our Biochemistry Analyzers are vital tools for modern diagnostics, designed to accurately measure key biological markers—such as enzymes, proteins, electrolytes, and metabolites—in blood, urine, and other fluids. They enable a wide range of tests essential for diagnosing illnesses, managing conditions, and tailoring patient care.

Built to meet international quality standards, the device ensures reliable, repeatable results for a wide range of diagnostic applications.
